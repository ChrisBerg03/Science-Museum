How can you support us?

Supporting us can be achieved through various means, such as becoming a member, volunteering your time and expertise, or making financial contributions. By actively participating in membership programs, attending events, or making donations, individuals can help sustain the museum's educational initiatives, promote scientific literacy, and ensure the continued growth of engaging exhibits and programs for the community.

Contact us

To contact us, please feel free to email us at science@mail.com or call our dedicated customer service line at +47 123 45 678.

Internship

Securing an internship at our science museum is a rewarding opportunity to immerse yourself in the dynamic world of science education and exhibition. To apply, please visit our official website and navigate to the "Careers" or "Internships" section, where you can find detailed information about available positions, application procedures, and requirements. We encourage prospective interns to submit a comprehensive application, including a resume, cover letter, and any relevant portfolios, showcasing their passion for science, education, and commitment to fostering a love for discovery among museum visitors. Our internship programs offer a unique chance to collaborate with experienced professionals, engage with diverse audiences, and contribute to the museum's mission of promoting scientific understanding and curiosity.
