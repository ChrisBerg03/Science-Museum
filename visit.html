Our primary goal at the science museum is to cultivate a profound appreciation for science ands foster a sense of wonder and curiosity among visitors of all ages. By providing engaging exhibits, interactive programs, and educational outreach initiatives, we aim to inspire a lifelong love for learning, empower the community with scientific knowledge, and contribute to the advancement of STEM education.

Admission

Entrance is free for all. Tours occur every hour, and cost 50 NOK. Contact us if you wish to have a tour in mass.

Accessibility

The museum has wheelchair accessibility and can arrange for other solutions if needed. Take contact with us if you need special assistance.
